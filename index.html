Pre-Launch Checklist for MealSight

BEFORE APPROVAL

Prepare ASO Assets

Optimize your App Store listing: refine keywords targeting "food scanner", "calorie tracker", "meal logging AI". Your screenshots look good — make sure the first 3 tell a clear story since those show on search results.

BEFORE APPROVAL

Pre-Seed Reviews

Line up 10-15 TestFlight beta users who can leave Day 1 reviews. Early ratings heavily influence your initial ranking and conversion rate.

LAUNCH WEEK

Cross-Promote from PatchPal

PatchPal has 130+ users who opted into a health-related app. Add a subtle in-app card or notification promoting MealSight — this is your warmest audience.

LAUNCH WEEK

Social Launch Campaign

Prepare a launch thread for X/Twitter, a Product Hunt listing, and Reddit posts in r/nutrition, r/loseit, r/fitness. Health/food apps do well with community-driven launches.

Strategic Overview

PatchPal is showing strong signals: 26.1% conversion rate is excellent (App Store average is ~3-5%), and 6.28 sessions per device indicates genuine engagement. The challenge is sustained discovery — 76.9% of downloads come from App Referrer (likely direct links you shared), with only 15.4% from organic App Store Search. Your growth lever is increasing organic discovery while maintaining that high conversion rate.

Immediate Actions (This Week)

HIGH IMPACT

ASO Overhaul for PatchPal

Only 15.4% of downloads come from App Store Search. Target keywords like "patch reminder", "medication patch tracker", "NRT tracker", "nicotine patch app". Add these to your subtitle and keyword field. Your 26% conversion rate means people who find you convert — you just need more people to find you.

Expected impact: 2-3x organic search downloads within 2-4 weeks
HIGH IMPACT

Replicate the Feb 24 Spike

55 downloads in one day from App Referrer — something worked. Was it a social post? A community share? Reddit? Identify what drove that spike and systematize it: schedule weekly posts, create shareable content, partner with health communities.

Expected impact: Recurring 20-30 download days instead of 1-5
REVENUE

Implement Monetization

$0 proceeds with 130 downloads and 6.28 sessions/device is a missed opportunity. Consider: a premium "insights" tier ($2.99/mo), a one-time unlock for advanced features ($4.99), or a "PatchPal Pro" subscription with detailed analytics and reminders.

Even 5% conversion at $2.99/mo = potential $19.40 MRR from current users
STABILITY

Fix All 4 Crashes

4 crashes in the reporting period isn't terrible, but for a health app, stability is trust. Each crash is a potential uninstall. Prioritize crash-free releases — aim for 99.9% crash-free sessions before scaling acquisition.

Expected impact: Improved retention and App Store rating

Medium-Term Plays (Next 2-4 Weeks)

GROWTH

Apple Search Ads — Start Small

With 26.1% conversion rate, paid search will be very efficient. Start with $5-10/day targeting your exact keywords. At this conversion rate, your CPI could be under $0.50, which is exceptional for iOS.

Expected: 10-20 additional daily installs at $5-10/day spend
ENGAGEMENT

Push Notification Strategy

6.28 sessions/device is great, but you need to convert that engagement into retention data. Implement smart reminders: "Time to apply your patch", "You're on day X — keep going!" Habit-forming notifications are key for health apps.

Expected: 20-30% improvement in Day 7 retention
EXPANSION

Expand Beyond US Market

US has 73 downloads (56%), but UK (12), Australia (8), Canada (7), and India (5) show demand. Localize your App Store listing for UK English, and consider targeting Australia/Canada with localized screenshots and descriptions.

Expected: 15-25% increase in total downloads
SYNERGY

Cross-Promote MealSight + PatchPal

Once MealSight launches, create a health ecosystem. Users quitting smoking often focus on diet — offer a bundle discount or cross-app integration. "Track your patch AND your nutrition recovery."

Expected: 20-40% of PatchPal users trying MealSight

Long-Term Vision (1-3 Months)

VIRALITY

Build Sharing Mechanics

Add milestone celebrations: "7 days patch-free!", "1 month milestone!" with beautiful shareable cards for social media. Each share = free App Referrer traffic (your strongest channel at 76.9%).

Expected: Self-sustaining organic growth loop
COMMUNITY

Health Community Partnerships

Partner with smoking cessation communities, NRT subreddits, health coaches, and pharmacies. Offer bulk promo codes. Health apps grow through trust — community endorsements are powerful.

Expected: Credibility + steady referral stream
PLATFORM

Widget + Apple Watch

A home screen widget showing "Days since last patch change" or "Next patch reminder" keeps PatchPal visible. Apple Watch complications for quick logging would differentiate you from competitors.

Expected: Higher engagement + App Store featuring potential
DATA

Health Insights Dashboard

Expected: Premium tier justification + lower churn
